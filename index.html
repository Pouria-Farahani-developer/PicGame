Rules of the game

1. let's start game by click on newgame button

2. By clicking on the button Roll dice, the dice of the program will start to roll

3. every time you roll the dice, the sum of your dice is saved in Current, and if the clear point dice comes, it clears all your points.

4. After each dice roll, you can save your points by click on hold button and give your opponent's turn

5. Any user whose total score reaches 100 or more is the winner of this game

Note: You can skip your turn and give it to your opponent by pressing the hold button
